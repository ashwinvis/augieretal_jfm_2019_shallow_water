.. figure:: ../Pyfig/fig_Kolmo_c=20_N=3840
   :alt:  Third order structure functions involved in the exact
   Kolmogorov law ([eq\_Kolmo]) averaged over a long simulation for
   :math:`c = 20` and :math:`n = 3840`. The structure functions are
   normalized by :math:`4 \eps r`. Black thick line,
   :math:`\mean{ \delta J_L|\delta \uu|^2 } 
   + c^2\mean{\delta u_L(\delta h)^2}`; light thin line,
   :math:`c^2\mean{\delta u_L (\delta h)^2}`; dark thin line,
   :math:`\mean{\delta J_L |\delta \uu|^2}`. The dotted straight line
   shows :math:`4 \eps_q r`, where :math:`\eps_q` is the quadratic
   energy dissipation rate.
   :width: 8.00000cm
